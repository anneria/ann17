City of Cape Town digital platform

The City Of Cape Town needed a platform that would align with their commitment of being the most digital city in Africa. Over the course of 3 years, Punk worked with them to achieve this goal.

The website was broken down to a template based front-end development system that could be handed over to the City’s development team and be integrated into the Sharepoint CMS.

Sanlam 1 of 1 Private Collection

Sanlam Private Wealth offers a wide range of bespoke financial services and products to their high net worth clients. The Private Collection digital portal is finely crafted to align with the goal of creating long-term relationships with clients and cater for their individual needs.

Morbo water photographer

Since 2013, Morné Loubscher has captured breathtaking moments of the ocean and the surf community around Western Cape, South Africa. His photography has become very popular, and he also sells his work in print format.

Morné wants to grow his brand beyond only social media, and this online presence is an ongoing collaboration to achieve his objectives. We are also working on designs for Morbo apparel.
